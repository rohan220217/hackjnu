Frequently Asked Questions

What's so special about your hack ?

Backed by the experienced hacker’s core team, we aim to make this event an inclusive one for each and every hacker out there. We are planning for special prizes and recognition for under-represented communitites in the world of tech!

How much do we charge ?

We believe in the culture of innovation and rewards for those who want to change the world! So, don’t worry about your food and logistics. That’s on us. You just have to hack and change the world!

Do we offer support ?

Unfortunately no. This is our first time conducting this hackathon. We would make sure that you have a good time when you reach here. Transportation cost is to be borne by you. Who knows, next time, we will sponser your travel too! <3

How to reach here ?

Nearest Airport is IGI Airport (12km). Nearest Metro station is R K PURAM

It is my first hackathon? Can I participate ?

Of course you can! Experience does not matter till the time you have the zest to change the world. New comers are most welcome!

Can I participate individually ?

Unfortunately, No. But then teams are fun! Min 2 participants and Maximum 4 participants. If you don’t have a team we will form it on spot!

What are the things that I need to bring with us ?

A valid Govt/College Id card, laptops, extension cords, hardware requirements(if any) and a great Idea to change the world! <3

Will food and accomodation be provided ?

Yes sure, absolutely.

Can I start working on my hack before the event ?

No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before the hackathon begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand.
